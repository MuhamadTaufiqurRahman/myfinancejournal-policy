My Finance Journal - Personal Finance Management App

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Financial Data: Transaction data you choose to enter (amounts, categories, dates, notes).
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services:

  • To authenticate your account and secure your data
  • To store and sync your financial transactions across devices
  • To generate financial reports and insights
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support

3. Data Storage & Security

Storage Location

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase servers with the following protections:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Firebase Realtime Database with security rules
  • Data encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With Google for authentication purposes
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Correction: Edit or update your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data
  • Export: Request export of your data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the app at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
